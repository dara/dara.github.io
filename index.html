Coffee Flavor Tree
2023

In 2014 I was frequently journaling my coffee tasting experiences. As my journal's pages were filling up, I envisioned an app that could replicate and expand on what my physical journal offered. Around the same time, I stumbled upon the fascinating concept of the coffee aroma wheel, which further fueled my imagination. It was then that I reached out to an ex-colleague, Seyfeddin, who had always shared a keen interest in innovative projects. He immediately came on board. Seyfeddin, in turn, brought Said Ozcan into the fold, and together they laid the groundwork for what would become the initial interactive prototype of our application, specifically designed for iOS, in 2016. However, as it often happens in life, our project took a pause. It wasn’t until recently that I decided to revisit and bring it partially to life using JavaScript.

Special acknowledgment is also due to Gizem Candemir, who contributed significantly by creating the first non-interactive prototype using the Principle app.
