Coffee Flavor Tree
2023

In 2014 I was frequently journaling my coffee tasting experiences. As my journal's pages were filling up, I envisioned an app that could replicate and expand on what my physical journal offered. Around the same time, I stumbled upon the fascinating concept of the coffee aroma wheel, which further fueled my imagination. We made an iOS app in 2016. However, as it often happens in life, our project took a pause. It wasn’t until recently that I decided to revisit and bring it partially to life using JavaScript.

Thanks to Seyfeddin and Said for the iOS app and special acknowledgment is also due to Gizem Candemir, who contributed significantly by creating the first non-interactive prototype using the Principle app.
